# Nota Orbis Vision

## Capabilities (Planned)

- **Ingestion**: Watch folders, transcribe audio (Whisper), OCR
- **Triage**: Categorize incoming items into PARA structure
- **Research**: Preemptive actions, information gathering
- **Review**: Surface items for periodic review
- **Curation**: Long-term organization and refinement
- **Archive**: Selection and retirement of inactive items

## Integrations

- **n8n**: Webhook triggers for automation workflows
- **Mealie**: Recipes, meal planning
- **Gemini**: Image generation
- **Local Whisper**: Audio transcription
